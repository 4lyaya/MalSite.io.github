My Journey in Tech

From building my first "Hello World" program to developing complex web applications, my passion for technology has been a constant driving force in my life. I'm Sarah Johnson, a full-stack developer with a knack for turning ideas into reality through code.

My journey began in my teens when I stumbled upon an old computer in my parents' garage. Curiosity led me to tear it apart and rebuild it, sparking a lifelong fascination with how things work in the digital world.

Today, I specialize in creating intuitive, user-friendly web applications that solve real-world problems. Whether it's streamlining business processes or crafting engaging user experiences, I'm always up for a challenge.
